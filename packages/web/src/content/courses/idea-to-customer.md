The difference between a junior engineer and a CTO isn't the mindset. It's the number of squares and the complexity of the trade-offs. The muscle is the same.

This curriculum is grounded in the SaaS business model across two stages of company maturity: early-stage startup and late-stage startup. You'll develop the discipline of ownership from day one using the Checkerboard model, the Conference Room method, and structured exercises designed to force genuine understanding — not surface-level familiarity.

Every hat (PM, Marketer, Sales Lead, CTO) is a lens on the same problem. Owning a square fully means considering it through each lens, knowing when to go deep, when familiarity is sufficient, and when to bring in a domain expert rather than pretending to be one.
