Product Managers obsess over problems, not solutions. Before any code is written, a good PM answers three questions: Who has this problem? How painful is it? Why haven't they already solved it?

In this module, you'll practice the PM mindset through real exercises — writing problem statements, conducting user interviews on paper, and producing a one-page PRD that any engineer could build from.
