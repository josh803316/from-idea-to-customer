Every bad project traces back to a framing failure, not a technical one. This module is the foundation everything else stands on.

You'll learn the Checkerboard model — the operating system for ownership at every level of seniority. You'll understand why the Conference Room method is the most effective learning tool most organizations have abandoned. And you'll start to see the distinction between bounded and unbounded problems, which is the single most clarifying lens for understanding why some work feels clear and other work feels impossible.

No assumptions about prior experience. The goal is the same whether you're three weeks into your first job or three years in: develop the muscle of genuine understanding over surface familiarity. Everything else compounds from here.
