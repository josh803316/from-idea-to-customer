The CTO's job isn't to pick the coolest technology — it's to pick the right technology for *this team*, *this problem*, and *this moment in time*. That requires understanding the business constraints as well as the technical ones.

In this module, you'll learn to evaluate tradeoffs, design a monorepo that teaches itself, and write the CLAUDE.md that makes your codebase instantly legible to any engineer.
