Marketing is frequently the most misunderstood function in engineering organizations. Engineers often conflate it with design, or dismiss it as the department that makes things look pretty. This module corrects that.

A marketer's job is to create demand. Everything else — the campaigns, the content, the SEO, the messaging — is in service of that goal. Understanding how demand gets created, and why engineering decisions directly impact a company's ability to generate it, changes how you think about every feature you ship.
